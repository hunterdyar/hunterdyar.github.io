About

I gave my Intro to Unity students about a week to make a video game. I took the challenge up myself and completed this in the same time period. It's a love letter to the old game, a personal favorite, Chips Challenge.

There's a lot of work to do, but it's a solid start and a decent proof-of-concept. I've taken what I learned and some of the systems from this game and used them in other sokabon-style projects, where it lives on.
